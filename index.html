Link

About Link

Link is a video game character from the Legend of Zelda. He protects Zelda and Hyrule Kingdom from the evil monster Ganon. He also carries the legendary Master Sword, Hylian Shield, and bow.

Characteristics

  1. He is brave and courageous
  2. He lives in Hyrule Kingdom
  3. He likes to ride a horse around Hyrule
